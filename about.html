about us

Safety Power is a pioneering venture that caters to the ever-growing and complex demands of the industry in the United Arab Emirates. With our extensive knowledge and experienced team, we excel in delivering projects of any size on time, even under challenging constraints and deadlines. Our comprehensive solutions encompass trading, equipment rental, and industrial support services. We back up our offerings with exceptional technical support provided by a highly trained and experienced management team. Their combined expertise and experience make a significant difference in our industry.

Policy

We have implemented a "Zero Harm, Zero Accident" policy to prioritize the safety of all our stakeholders. We take great pride in creating a safe working environment with robust safety systems. Furthermore, we provide growth opportunities for our stakeholders to realize their full potential. Our policies, embedded within the Company's Management System, undergo regular internal reviews to ensure their continued relevance and effectiveness.

Our mission

Our mission is to meet our clients' demands by providing the highest level of quality services at fair and competitive prices. We aim to foster the growth of our company through repeat and referral business based on customer satisfaction in all aspects, including adherence to timelines, attention to detail, and a service-centric approach. We strive to achieve and maintain the highest levels of professionalism, integrity, honesty, and fairness in our relationships with all our stakeholders, including employees, suppliers, subcontractors, professional associates, and customers.
